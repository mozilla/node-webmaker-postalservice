Thank you for registering a Maker Party event. We're excited that you're joining us!

The key to a successful Maker Party event is planning, so we've put together a checklist to help you along the way:

Before your event

  • Visit our Get Involved page for everything you need to throw your party.
  • Start planning using these helpful event guides.
  • Request a free Maker Party gear pack that includes stickers, t-shirts and banners.
  • Reach out to media with our press kit.
  • Questions? Visit our FAQ.

During your event

  • Remember to document everything with photos and video!
  • Encourage your participants to show off what they make with this shareable template.
  • Help improve the Mozilla Webmaker project by conducting user testing during your event.

After your event

  • Share the details of your party on Twitter with the #makerparty hashtag.
  • Host another event! The global Maker Party runs until September 15.

If you have questions, ideas for collaboration or information you'd like to share with us, don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help.
